My all time favorite movies

10 things i hate about you

10 things i hate about you is a 1999 American movie that talks a lot about the differnt aspect of one of my favorite subjects which is literature. It is such a heartwarming movie and the genre of the movie is Romance

The movie is about a typical bad boy (Patrick) who falls for a girl (Kat) who he was paid to date and dump at the end of the school year, The film is named after a poem Kat writes about her romance with Patrick.

It's okay not to be okay

It's okay not to be okay is a korean movie that i absolutely love, even though i haven't watched a lot of korean movies of all the ones i've watched It's okay not to be okay is the best one yet. The genre of the movie is Romance and Drama

The movie is about an anti social children's book author who basically moved back to her hometown where she avoided for years just becuase of her love interest who is a nurse in a psychiatrist hospital. I just love how unconventional the movie is. It is basically different from anything i have ever watched

Kuch Kuch Hota Hai

Kuch Kuch Hota Hai is an indian movie that i love so much, I was probably like 7 when i first watched this movie and i have watched it many other times after that because of how much i like it. The meaning of the movie translates to "Something happens in my heart". The genre of the movie is obviously romance

This movie is about a girl that sets out to reunite her father with the college best friend who loved him as per her mother's last wish only to discover the woman is engaged.The movie is a perfect mixture of romance and comedy and that's what i love most about it.

Hercai

Hercai is a Turkish dizi(Soap Opera) that i recently completed, It is 69 episodes long and got annoying at some point but it was still a very amazing drama overall. I really enjoyed watching it although i can't watch it over again. The genre is Romance and Revenge.

The movie is about Miran is a mysterious man who appears in the life of Reyyan, a young woman from a wealthy and very powerful family, and she falls in love. But things don't turn out as she expected, as there is a dark motive behind everything.
